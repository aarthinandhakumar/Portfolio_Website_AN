I, Aarthi, welcome you to visit my portfolio


I am a Masters student in Computer Information Systems at Boston University, specializing in web development and user experience design. Throughout my studies, I have delved into various aspects of computer science, from algorithms and data structures to software engineering and artificial intelligence.


As a budding web developer, my journey is one of constant creation and innovation. Each project presents a unique canvas upon which I apply my skills and expertise, crafting intuitive and engaging experiences for users across the globe.


My role involves problem solving, tasked with troubleshooting issues, optimizing performance, and ensuring the seamless functionality of every digital creation. Whether it's debugging a complex piece of code, optimizing website speed and performance, or ensuring compatibility across different browsers and devices, I approach each challenge with precision, ingenuity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.


Beyond the world of code, I'm also deeply passionate about two other creative pursuits: blogging and photography. Being a foodie and a lover of animals, i focus my blogging and clicking skills when i get to relish my favorite food and while i see random cute animals.


Feel free to explore my little universe, where you can find more about my projects, my blogs and gallery. I am positive it will make you smile.
